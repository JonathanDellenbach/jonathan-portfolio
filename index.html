About Me

I'm Jonathan - a recent graduate of SETU Carlow's BSc (Hons) Computer Games Development programme. Over four years I've built everything from pathfinding visualisers and AI steering behaviours in C++ to a full mobile game with a five-person team.

When I'm not working on coursework, I'm usually messing around on side projects with a couple of friends - half the time it starts out as a joke and turns into an actual working thing a few weekends later.
