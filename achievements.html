Ping for Peace - National Ploughing Championships

Over one weekend, I was tasked with remaking Flappy Bird as a small mobile game for a stand representing peace and joy. Built in Godot, it ran on smartphones at the National Ploughing Championships, where people stopped by to play it as part of a "Ping for Peace" initiative.
